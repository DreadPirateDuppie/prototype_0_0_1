Privacy Policy for Pushinn

Last Updated: November 27, 2024

Introduction

Pushinn ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using Pushinn, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Display name, bio, and profile picture
  • User Content: Skateboarding posts, videos, photos, comments, and ratings you share
  • Battle Information: Your participation in skateboarding battles and related data

Automatically Collected Information

  • Location Data: Your precise location when you enable location services to:
    • View and create location-based skateboarding posts
    • Discover nearby skate spots
    • Share your location with other users (optional)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features you use, time spent in the app, interactions with content
  • Log Data: IP address, crash reports, and performance data

Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication Data: Information from authentication providers (Supabase Auth)
  • Advertising Data: Anonymous advertising identifiers for ad delivery

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Enable you to discover and share skateboarding content
  • Show you location-based skate spots and posts
  • Facilitate battles and community features
  • Award points and manage the rewards system
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Communicate with you about the App
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

Location Services

Pushinn uses your location to:

  • Display nearby skate spots on the map
  • Allow you to create location-tagged posts
  • Show you relevant local content
  • Enable location sharing with other users (optional)

You can control location permissions through your device settings. Disabling location services will limit certain features but the App will remain functional.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Share Your Information With:

Supabase (Database & Authentication)

  • We use Supabase to store user data, posts, and app content
  • Data is stored securely on Supabase servers
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Google Services

  • Google Maps: For displaying maps and location data
  • Google AdMob: For serving advertisements
  • Google Analytics (if applicable): For app usage analytics
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Other Users

  • Your public profile information, posts, and location-tagged content are visible to other users
  • You can control what location information you share in app settings

We Do Not:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your precise location without your explicit permission
  • Share your email address publicly

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device information
  • Usage data
  • Location data (for location-based ads)

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

Learn more: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543
