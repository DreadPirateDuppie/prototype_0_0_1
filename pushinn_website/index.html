> PROJECT_INFO_

Built by Riders, for Riders

Pushinn emerged from the need for a dedicated platform where skateboarders and BMX riders could connect, compete, and share their passion. Traditional social media platforms weren't built for our community's unique needs.

Our mission is to create the ultimate digital skate spot - where every trick matters, every rider has a voice, and the community drives the experience.
